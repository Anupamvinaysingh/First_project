About Us

The Institute Cultural Council is a community of students passionate about innovation, arts, and collaboration. It organises different cultural activities like traditional dance and music, modern dance and music, filming, photography, etc. We host events and workshops to help members explore their creative potential and develop skills in design, writing, photography, and more.
